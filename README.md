# Pizza-Sales-Project
Purpose: Analyze pizza sales data to understand sales trends, customer preferences, and product performance across different regions and time periods.

Data Preparation: Cleaned and transformed raw sales data for accurate insights and reporting.

Dashboard Features:
Monthly and yearly sales trends visualization
Top-selling pizza types and sizes
Customer ordering patterns and preferences
Regional sales comparison

Tools Used: Power BI, Power Query, DAX, Excel

Business Impact: Helps optimize inventory, improve marketing strategies, and drive sales growth by providing actionable insights.
